Blockify Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Blockify, developed by Agustín Louro Paz, collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Core Functionality and Sensitive Permissions

We declare that access to these permissions is essential for the Application's core functionality:

  • Call Log (READ_CALL_LOG / WRITE_CALL_LOG): Real-time number identification and history management.
  • Contacts (READ_CONTACTS): Local "Whitelist" management.
  • Call Management (READ_PHONE_STATE / ANSWER_PHONE_CALLS): Effective detection and blocking of calls.

Note: Data processing is strictly local. We do not share or sell your information.

2. Backups

Backups are local and stored on your device. There is no synchronization with our own servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics for anonymous technical error reporting.

4. International Compliance (GDPR, CCPA, Australia)

We respect data access and deletion rights. This application is not intended for persons under the age of 16.
